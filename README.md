## 💜 about

we’re a two-person digital café from myanmar.  
we build clean, dark-mode-first websites for small businesses.  
no fluff. no ghosting. just warm, reliable code.

- 🐝 **bebee** — developer, architect, tea drinker  
- 🛡️ **arthur** — sales, qa, shield of the hive

## 💙 what we make

- **websites & catalogues** — fast, mobile-ready, translation-friendly  
- **booking tools** — simple calendars for clinics, salons, cafés  
- **inventory trackers** — turning messy spreadsheets into calm dashboards  

everything comes with dark mode, reusable baseplates, and a built-in 5% charity share.
